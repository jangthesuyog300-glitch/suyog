Information of Trees

trees are one of the most important parts of nature. They give us oxygen, which we need to breathe, and take in carbon dioxide, helping to keep the air clean. Trees also provide shade, fruits, wood, and shelter to many animals and birds.

Trees help prevent soil erosion and keep the environment cool. They are also beautiful to look at and make our surroundings peaceful and green. People often plant trees in parks, gardens, and along roads to improve the environment.

Without trees, life on Earth would be very difficult. That is why we must protect trees and plant more of them. Saving trees means saving life.
